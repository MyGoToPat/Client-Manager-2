# HiPat Lead Generation & Client Onboarding System

## Strategic Overview

The "Add New Client" button is actually the entry point to a **lead generation system**. Each tool serves two purposes:

1. **Capture valuable data** about a prospect
2. **Create a reason to sign up for Pat** (to save/view their results)

---

## Two Modes for Every Tool

Each lead gen tool should work in **two modes**:

| Mode | Who Fills It Out | Use Case |
|------|------------------|----------|
| **Self-Service** | Prospect fills it out themselves | Link on website, social media, QR at gym |
| **Live Consultation** | Mentor fills it out WITH client | In-person consult, Zoom call, gym floor |
